About

Do you feel you don’t have time to ensure your child’s mastery of math concept taught by school? Do you want to lift your child’s math aptitude above grade level?

Our classroom is committed to provide all year round math instruction to elementary or middle school students. Students can benefit from the systematic review of the materials already learned from school and the preview of new math concepts.

Having tutored my daughter math for more than three years, I understand how to motivate and inspire children to excel in math and how to help them develop good study habits and discipline.

Students will be instructed in small groups based on their individual skill levels. Our classroom will focus on helping students to become proficient problem-solvers, develop critical thinking skills and gain a deep understanding of core mathematics concepts.
